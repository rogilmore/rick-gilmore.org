Appointments

I am Associate Professor of Psychology at Penn State. I serve on the Psychology Department's Planning and Advisory Committee, and am a member of both the Cognitive and Developmental areas. In addition, I serve on the executive committee for the Specialization in Cognitive and Affective Neuroscience (SCAN), on the Social Sciences Research Institute Internal Advisory Board, and as a Child Study Center initiative leader. From 2008 to 2014, I served as Director of Human Imaging at Penn State's Social, Life, & Engineering Sciences Imaging Center (SLEIC). My appointment is co-funded by the Huck Institutes of the Life Sciences. {% endblock content %}
