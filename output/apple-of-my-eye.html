Apple of my eye

Wed 27 August 2014 by Rick Gilmore

I'm on the Megabus to New York. It's time to check in with the Databrary and Datavyu project teams. We're hiring, by the way. So, if you are a UI/UX or JavaScript developer, please consider applying.

We can't wait to open the site to the general public later this fall. We already have a set of fascinating video data and dynamic displays from a wide range of researchers. If you are a researcher who wants to share data, consider registering for the private beta.

Of course, there are benefits to having a project that requires periodic travel to the Big Apple. Does anyone call it that anymore? The capital of the world, a French friend once called it. Or, the City. Whatever you call it, I enjoy going. And, I enjoy working with these colleagues on this project a great deal.
